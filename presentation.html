Four pillars to coordinate reform, mobilise capital, and rebuild Britain’s primary markets.

Education & Participation

  • Funded education platforms to close the UK/US content gap

  • Connect PISCES → IPO → retail pathway

Recommendation

Call to Action

The FCA’s 2025–2030 strategy explicitly prioritises growth.26 A Primary Capital Core Policy Framework operationalises that strategic commitment — and provides the coordinating mechanism the FCA itself needs.

  1. Endorse a coordinating framework
    Connecting all 10+ reform workstreams into a coherent primary capital pipeline — from private markets (PISCES) through listing to retail access.
  2. Fast-track the targeted support regime
    For retail education platforms, enabling personalised tools without full advice authorisation — closing the advice gap at scale.
  3. Mandate structured disclosure
    To democratise market information — replacing unstructured Word documents with machine-readable data at source, reducing asymmetry between institutional and retail investors.
